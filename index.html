Pastebot for Mac

Pastebot is your intelligent clipboard manager. It captures everything you copy, organizes it into custom pasteboards, and lets you recall items instantly from anywhere. Build filters with live previews, assign keyboard shortcuts, and sync seamlessly across your Macs. Invisible when not needed, indispensable when it is.

Effortless Clipboard History

Everything you copy is saved automatically. Search, preview, and filter clippings by content, app, date, or type before pasting. Paste in sequence or revert to previous items with a shortcut.

Custom Pasteboards and Shortcuts

Group frequent clippings into organized pasteboards. Assign global keyboard shortcuts to pasteboards or specific items. Access them from any app, even in full-screen or split-view modes.

Powerful Filters

Create filters with live previews to transform text, images, or data as you paste. Apply them instantly via shortcuts and export to share with others. Always paste plain text if preferred.

Seamless Sync and Privacy

iCloud syncs your clipboard, pasteboards, and filters across all Macs. Blacklist apps to exclude unwanted copies. Works alongside macOS Universal Clipboard for iOS integration.

Keyboard-Driven Workflow

Dozens of shortcuts for power users: paste last item, apply recent filter, quick search. Release-to-paste and customizable quick paste windows adapt to your style.
